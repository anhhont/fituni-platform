Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

1. Introduction

FitUni, developed as a student project, is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with applicable data protection laws and university policies. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when using our platform.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following data, in compliance with data protection laws:

  • Basic Data: University email, name (optional), gender, profile description (bio).
  • Sensitive Data: Sports preferences (e.g., badminton, yoga), skill levels, available times, location (e.g., Campus, Downtown).
  • Technical Data: IP address, session data (login sessions, anonymized analytics).

3. How We Collect Data

Data is collected when you:

  • Sign up or sign in using your university email and password.
  • Complete your profile (sports, availability, location).
  • Interact on the platform (send match requests, accept/reject matches).
  • Receive verification codes or notifications via email.

4. Purpose and Legal Basis

We process your data with your consent to:

  • Create and verify your account (university email, password).
  • Match you with workout partners based on preferences, availability, and location.
  • Send email notifications (verification codes, match updates).
  • Ensure security (bcrypt encryption, bot detection).
  • Perform anonymized analytics (no personal data shared).
  • Comply with university policies and applicable laws.

By clicking "Sign Up" or signing in, you consent to this processing.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is securely stored in a cloud database with the following security measures:

  • Password encryption using bcrypt.
  • Honeypot fields to detect bots.
  • Temporary verification codes (expire after 1 hour).

Data is retained only as long as necessary or as required by law.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data, except:

  • With matched workout partners (name, sports, availability).
  • As required by university authorities or legal obligations.
  • Anonymized data for analytics (not linked to individuals).

The platform does not use third-party advertising or analytics services.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access, modify, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Request data transfer or restrict processing.
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities if data is misused.

Contact: support@example.edu.

8. Data Transfer

The platform operates within the university community and does not transfer data to third parties for other purposes.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updates. Check this page regularly.

10. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact: support@example.edu.
